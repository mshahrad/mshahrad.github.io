TTCN: Trust to the Truthful Check Node

This was my first research paper which came out of my senior thesis as an undergrad at Sharif University of Technology. The work is on increasing the efficiency of a particular class of low-density parity-check (LDPC) decoders through early termination of decoding iterations. This is achieved by finding a "Truthful" processing unit and sharing its results with other processing nodes in early decoding iterations. TTCN improves the energy efficiency and throughput of split-row LDPC decoders.

TTCN: A new approach for low-power split-row LDPC decoders
Mohammad Shahrad, Mahdi Shabany
IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems (ISCAS), 2015
