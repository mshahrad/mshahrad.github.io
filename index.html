About Me

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Princeton's Department of Electrical Engineering, and a member of the Princeton Parallel Research Group advised by Professor David Wentzlaff.
My work focuses on building more efficient large-scale computing systems by merging Computer Architecture, Computer Systems, and Economics. I am an enthusiastic hands-on engineer with experience in multiple layers of abstraction, from circuits to large-scale cloud systems.
