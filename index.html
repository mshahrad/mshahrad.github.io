I am an Assistant Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of British Columbia (UBC). At the confluence of systems and architecture, my research focuses on building more efficient large-scale computing systems. I am an enthusiastic, hands-on researcher with experience in multiple layers of abstraction, from circuits to large-scale cloud systems. My primary research domain is cloud resource management, more recently in the context of serverless systems. I lead the UBC Cloud Infrastructure Research for Reliability, Usability, and Scalability (CIRRUS) Lab.
